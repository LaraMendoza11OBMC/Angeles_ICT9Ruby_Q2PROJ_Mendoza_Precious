INGREDIENTS

8 ounces extra firm tofu fried
37 grams Knorr Crab and Corn Soup
12 quail eggs boiled
1 ½ cups cauliflower florets
1 ½ cups broccoli florets
1 carrot
15 snow peas
1 red bell pepper
1 green bell pepper
1 ½ cups cabbage
1 yellow onion
4 cloves garlic
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon oyster sauce
1 ½ cups water
Salt and ground black pepper to taste
3 tablespoons cooking oil

STEPS

1. Heat oil in a wok. Sauté onion and garlic until the onion softens.


2. Add carrot , cauliflower, and broccoli. Cook for 2 minutes.


3. Add cabbage, bell pepper, and snow peas. Toss.


4. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce and continue cooking for 2 minutes.


5. Combine water and Knorr Crab and Corn Soup Mix. Stir and pour into the wok. Let it boil.


6. Add fried tofu and quail eggs. Continue cooking until the sauce reduces and thickens.


7. Season with salt and ground black pepper as needed.
